Delphi

Harvesting Patentable Innovations

At most, if not all, technology corporations, IP lawyers are forced to rely on an engineer to recognize the potential patentability of an invention and submit an invention disclosure. Not only does this result in many missed opportunities for patent filing, it also results in the unnecessary review of un-patentable invention disclosures and the need for time-consuming “harvest” sessions where IP lawyers spend hours trying to determine which projects consist of patentable inventions. Delphi solves this problem by automatically extracting potential inventions and evaluating them for patentability, offering a high-level score that protects in-house counsel from willful infringement while also giving them an accurate metric of novelty and value.
